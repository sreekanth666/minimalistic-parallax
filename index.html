Principles

Embrace simplicity by eliminating unnecessary elements and focusing on what is essential for a clean and uncluttered look.

Utilize clean and straight lines in design elements to create a sense of order and simplicity.

Opt for a limited color palette, often centered around neutral tones, to achieve a harmonious and calming atmosphere.

Prioritize functionality in design, ensuring that every element serves a purpose, promoting practicality and efficiency.
